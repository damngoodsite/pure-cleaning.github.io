Construction Cleaning

Pure provides construction phase cleaning that removes all construction related debris from any area. Pure is flexible and can complete this process periodically, as needed, or throughout the construction process. If you have a smaller remodel that was done or small construction project we are your choice.

Services we provide include:

  • Detailed cleaning of decorative finishes
  • Interior & exterior glass cleaning
  • Deep cleaning
  • Preventative maintenance
  • Maintenance of a model home after staging
